Questions

Good to know

Is this really different from Notion or a to-do app?

Most tools assume you'll show up with willpower. Project ADHD is built around how ADHD brains work: frictionless capture so nothing's lost, gentle structure that doesn't overwhelm, and a reward system that gives your brain the dopamine it needs to keep going.

Do I need to set up a complicated system?

No. Start by dumping thoughts. Templates and projects are there when you want them, ignorable when you don't. It works whether you're a planner or a "I'll deal with it later" person.

What does the XP and coins stuff actually do?

You earn XP and coins for completing actions, habits and projects. Coins can be redeemed for rewards you set yourself, and XP levels up your character — turning everyday tasks into something your brain wants to do.

How much will it cost?

It's free during early access. Waitlist members get the longest free run and a say in what gets built next.

Where does my data live?

Project ADHD runs in your browser and keeps your data with you. No selling, no creepy tracking — your brain dump is your business.
